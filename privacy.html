Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Overview

LetsEatOut.ai ("we," "our," or "us") operates a restaurant directory website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website at letseatout.ai.

Information We Collect

Information you provide: If you submit a restaurant listing or contact us, you may provide business name, address, phone number, and email address.

Automatically collected information: Like most websites, we collect standard log data including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages. This is collected through Cloudflare, our hosting provider.

Location data: If you use our "Near Me" feature, we request your location with your permission to show nearby restaurants. This data is processed locally in your browser and is not stored on our servers.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Display restaurant listings and directory information
  • Respond to inquiries and listing submissions
  • Improve our website and user experience
  • Analyze aggregate traffic patterns

Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for website functionality. Cloudflare may set security cookies to protect against malicious traffic. We do not use advertising tracking cookies or sell your data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses:

  • Cloudflare: For hosting, security, and performance optimization
  • Google Fonts: For typography (subject to Google's privacy policy)

Data Retention

We retain restaurant listing information indefinitely to maintain our directory. Contact form submissions are retained as needed to respond to inquiries. Log data is retained according to Cloudflare's standard policies.

Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Access information we have about you
  • Correct inaccurate listing information
  • Remove your restaurant listing from our directory

To make a request, email us at ads@letseatout.ai.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at ads@letseatout.ai.
